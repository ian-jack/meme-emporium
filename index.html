Welcome to the Meme Emporium

Memes have gained immense popularity on the internet over the last decade, though they have been around far longer than some may realize. Dilligent efforts have been made to catalouge memes as they have grown and evolved, one of more notable being Know Your Meme, which exhaustively documents memes and their origins. This sit is not meant to be nearly as comprehensive, rather, it serves as more of a hall of fame for memes current, aging, or long-dead. Initially it will have only a basic gallery, but in later revisions visitors can expect more detailed information on each meme, a random meme generator, and even the ability to create and post their own memes.
