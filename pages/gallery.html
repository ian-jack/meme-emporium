Gallery

The Gallery is sorted into three categories: current memes, dated memes, and founding memes. Each will be further described below.

Current Memes

It can be hard to determine when a meme truly came into being. Some can go largely unnoticed for months or years until they find the right audience. As such, "current" memes are considered memes that are current trending on the internet, regardless of their original debut.

Dated Memes

Dated memes are a mixed bunch. Some still find sporadic use because of their ability to present a certain sensation. Others are so old that their age becomes something of a meme in itself. While some memes truly die and are likely to never catch attention again, many whose day has long passed are remembered fondly and used to elicit feelings of nostalgia.

Founding Memes

Some of the earliest intances of internet memes can be linked to these images. Many, specifically those that appear hand-drawn, originate from a 4-Chan trend called Rage Comics. Others just happend to be some of the first memes to ever go viral. A lot has changed since the advent of these iconic memes, but they will always hold a special status of being among the first, and will never truly die.
